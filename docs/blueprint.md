# **App Name**: AgroMachines Equipments

## Core Features:

- Farming Gear Concierge: A tool that helps farmers identify the right equipment based on their specific soil type, crop variety, and acreage.
- LeadGen Hero Section: High-impact brand landing with WhatsApp-integrated 'Enquire Now' CTA button for direct business conversion.
- Visual Product Gallery: An 8-item catalog grid featuring detailed technical specs for the Regular Model, HD Rotavator, Laser Land Leveler, and other machinery.
- B2B Transparency Factsheet: Clean presentation of manufacturing business credentials including GST, IEC code, and payment terms to build trust.
- Universal Inquiry Tracker: A centralized lead capture system for the contact form and WhatsApp API interactions using an external endpoint.
- Responsive Social Proof: Customer testimonial carousel featuring high-quality visuals of the equipment in use across various Punjab farming districts.
- Single-Page Smooth Navigation: Hash-based routing with a sticky header to ensure mobile users can quickly find company and product details.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Agricultural Green (#2D5A27) symbolizing growth and mechanical reliability.
- Accent color: Harvest Gold (#A18933) to represent ripe Punjab crops and quality performance.
- Background color: Earthy Mist (#F4F7F4), a heavily desaturated light tint for a clean professional aesthetic.
- Font pairing: 'Alegreya' (serif) for headlines to establish manufacturing heritage, and 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for readable technical specs.
- Custom outline icons for value propositions, focused on themes of durability, efficiency, and support.
- Mobile-first, single-page architecture using distinct banded sections to organize corporate facts, products, and contact info.
- Subtle slide-up entry animations for product cards to engage mobile scrollers without affecting page load speeds.
